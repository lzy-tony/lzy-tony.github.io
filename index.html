Biography

I am a Ph.D. student at the Department of Automation at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Gao Huang. I received my B.E. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University in July, 2024.

Previously, I was a research intern at Visual Computing Group, Microsoft Research Asia from September, 2023 to May, 2024. I was a research intern at Foundation Model Group, Megvii Research from May, 2023 to October, 2023. I was a research intern at RealAI from July, 2022 to October, 2022. I was a research assistant at TSAIL Group from December, 2021 to September, 2022.

Currently, my research interest includes topics on deep generative models and their applications in Computer Vision and Embodied AI.
